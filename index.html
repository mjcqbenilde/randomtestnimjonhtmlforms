About BPC

The Benildean Press Corps (BPC) is the official student-journalists’ organization of De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde, cultivating the next generation of journalists and artists. From writing articles that spark conversations to designing pages that pop, BPC is all about creativity and expression!

At the heart of BPC is The Benildean, the College’s official student magazine-journal. But that’s not all! Each year, BPC unleashes an explosion of creativity through other initiatives: Dekunstrukt (photography folio), Horizons (art folio), and Shades of Gray (literary folio). It’s all happening here at BPC—where every story shines and every voice matters!
